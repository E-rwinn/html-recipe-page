Churros with Cinnamon Sugar

Servings: 4â€“6 | Time: ~45 mins

Ingredients:

For dough:

  1. 1 cup water
  2. 2 tbsp sugar
  3. 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 2 tbsp butter
  5. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  6. 2 eggs
  7. 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

For frying and coating:

  1. Oil for frying
  2. 1/2 cup sugar + 1 tsp cinnamon (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring water, sugar, salt, and butter to a boil.
  2. Turn off heat, stir in flour until a dough forms.
  3. Let cool a bit, then beat in eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Transfer to piping bag with star tip.
  5. Heat oil in deep pan. Pipe strips of dough into oil and fry until golden.
  6. Drain, then roll in cinnamon sugar.
  7. Serve with chocolate sauce or dulce de leche if desired.
