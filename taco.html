Tacos al Pastor (Marinated Pork Tacos)

Servings: 4 | ‚è± Time: ~1 hour + marinating time

üå∂Ô∏è Ingredients ‚Äì Marinade

  1. 1 lb (450g) pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  2. 2 dried guajillo chilies (seeds removed)
  3. 2 garlic cloves
  4. 1/2 onion
  5. 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  6. 2 tbsp vinegar
  7. 1 tsp cumin
  8. 1/2 tsp oregano
  9. 1 tsp paprika
  10. Salt to taste

Ingredients ‚Äì For Serving

  1. Corn tortillas
  2. 1 cup diced pineapple
  3. Chopped onion and cilantro
  4. Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Soak dried chilies in hot water for 10 min. Blend with garlic, onion, pineapple juice, vinegar, and spices until smooth.
  2. Marinate pork in the sauce for at least 1 hour (overnight is best).
  3. Grill or pan-fry pork until browned.
  4. Warm tortillas, fill with pork, pineapple, onion, and cilantro. Serve with lime.
