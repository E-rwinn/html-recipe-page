Tacos al Pastor

Marinated Pork Tacos

🍽 Servings: 4 | ⏱ Time: ~1 hour + marinating time

🧂 Ingredients

Marinade:

  • 1 lb (450g) pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  • 2 dried guajillo chilies (seeds removed)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt to taste

For serving:

  • Corn tortillas
  • 1 cup diced pineapple
  • Chopped onion and cilantro
  • Lime wedges

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Soak dried chilies in hot water for 10 min. Blend with garlic, onion, pineapple juice, vinegar, and spices until smooth.
  2. Marinate pork in the sauce for at least 1 hour (overnight is best).
  3. Grill or pan-fry pork until browned.
  4. Warm tortillas, fill with pork, pineapple, onion, and cilantro. Serve with lime.
