Mexican Rice (Arroz Rojo)

Servings: 4 | ⏱ Time: ~30 minutes

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup long grain white rice
  2. 2 tbsp oil
  3. 1/4 onion, chopped
  4. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  5. 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  6. 1/2 cup tomato sauce or blended tomato
  7. 1/4 cup peas (optional)
  8. Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice under cold water until it runs clear.
  2. In a saucepan, heat oil. Add rice and toast until lightly golden.
  3. Add onion and garlic. Sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato sauce and broth. Stir and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. Add peas in the last 5 minutes if using. Fluff and serve.
