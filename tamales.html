  4. 1/2 tsp cumin
  5. Salt to taste
  6. 2 cups chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Prepare the chicken: Cook and shred chicken. Saut√© onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Make red chile sauce: Soak chiles in hot water for 15 minutes. Blend with garlic, cumin, salt, and chicken broth until smooth. Strain and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix shredded chicken with red chile sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Prepare masa dough: Beat lard until fluffy. Mix masa harina, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add chicken broth and mix. Fold in whipped lard until light and fluffy. Dough should float in water when ready.
  5. Drain soaked corn husks. Spread about 2 tablespoons of masa on each husk, add 1 tablespoon of chicken filling, then fold husks to enclose.
  6. Steam tamales upright for about 1.5 to 2 hours, adding water as needed.
  7. Let tamales rest for a few minutes before unwrapping and serving.
